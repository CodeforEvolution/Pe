Preferences

The preferences panel of Pe is quite extensive and therefore deserves a chapter on its own. This dialog is a so-called multi-pane dialog. If you click an item in the list on the left-hand side, the corresponding pane will be displayed.

The preferences will be operative from the moment you click Apply. Choose Revert to revert to the last-saved settings.

These are the panes contained in this dialog:

  • Colours
  • Connections
  • Differences
  • Editor
  • File Types
  • Files
  • Glossary
  • Grep Patterns
  • Groups
  • Include Paths
  • Invisibles
  • Keybindings
  • Languages
  • Searching
  • Startup
  • State
  • Worksheet
  • Wrapping
